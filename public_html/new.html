So you are an interested adventurer but need some help ....

Above all else, ready the Policy and understand it

Connecting to the MUD:
You need a program to connect to the Server the MUD is running on through the Internet

MUD Terms:
MOB - short for MOBILE, these are the creatures you are seeking to destroy
EQ - short for EQUIPMENT
TIN TIN/ZMUD - specialized MUD clients to connect to the server, they allow for color and autoactions

MOB Attributes: Why did my character die:
MOBs have many attributes, much like your character does. Nothing is more frustrating than to die from something you did not even see.
AGGRESSIVE - some mobs will attack you as you enter a room (or perhaps standing in it), for no other reason other than you are there. There is also AGG_GOOD, AGG_EVIL and AGG_NEUTRAL where these MOBS will attack you if you are of this alignment
HIDDEN - You the character will not see the MOB by normal means, coupled with the AGGRESSIVE flag, this can be devastating for a new player
INVISIBLE - You the character will not see the MOB by normal means and is different from HIDDEN, but can have the same results
MEMORY - Some MOBS will remember that you attacked them and will defend themselves immediately against you until you have killed it or it has killed you. It can be the case where you have attacked a MOB and escaped. You later die, but not at that MOBs hands, so it will still try to kill you if it encounters you again. This of course will discontinue if the game resets
LOOT - These MOBs will pick up items it finds on the ground (perhaps it killed you?). If you did not kill the MOB with all of your eq on, imagine trying with less of your eq.
Special Processes - Through coding MOBS can do a number of devastating things like inflict damage on particular classes or persons fighting it. Most anything can happen, do not be too surprised. This is generally on the tougher MOBs in the game.

Player Attributes:
Strength - how strong your character is
Intelligence - how smart your character is
Wisdom - how well your character uses his intelligence
Dexterity - how well your character moves
Constitution - the stamina your character has
Charisma - How "good looking" your character is, or the natural ability your character has for persons to admire them
Level - What level your character is
Alignment - If you kill evil things, in general you are good and vice versa.
Size - how tall your character is
Weight - how much your character weighs

Item Attributes:
Items modify your character when you use them. It is specific for each item in the game. You may need to identify things before using them

General Tips:
Carry a scroll of recall with you at all times! This may get you out of fights or help you if you are lost
Stick to Newbie areas at first until you get used to how your character will work
Do not be afraid to ask for help, in general most people will help you
Do not continuously ask people for help, do some exploring on your own
Before you attack a MOB, type "con MOB_NAME", it will give you an estimation of what may happen if you are completely healthy
Remember it is only a game, play to have fun
