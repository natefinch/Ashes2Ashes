Ashes to Ashes is a MUD or a text based adventure / role playing game.
You can play Ashes to Ashes by telneting to mud.digital-genesis.com port 3333

Ashes to Ashes features 9 character classes:
Warriors - Strong (but not too bright), they do a lot of damage in battle
Paladins - Subclass of good Warriors (though not as fierce fighters), balanced with some Cleric abilities
Anti-Paladins - Subclass of evil Warriors (though not as fierce fighters), balanced with some Magic User abilities
Rangers - Subclass of Warriors, excellent trackers / woodsmen and woodswomen
Monks - Adventures with unique ability to defend themselves in combat
Thieves - Adventurers with special talents
Clerics - Adventurers with little to no fighting skills, but heal (and many other spells) those in fights
Magic Users - Adventures with little to no fighting skills, typically weak, but cast devastating spells
Psionicist - Strong telepaths that can do many unique things with their minds

Ashes to Ashes features equipment galore for those who seek their fortune:
The equipment you find at first, is not near as good as the equipment you will earn in a Quest as you adventure at Ashes to Ashes.

Special Features you will find at Ashes to Ashes: (beyond normal shops)
- A shop with a person that can repair items damaged in fights
- A shop with a person who can remove invisibility from objects adventurers keep losing because they forget about the invisible items.
- A shop that sells Quest Equipment! Yes it is no easy task to achieve, but fun to window shop while you continue in Quests to earn the points needed.
